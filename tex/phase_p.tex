Phase~A2 showed that the raw angle field is rotational. Here we use the more relevant scalar: the full Meta-MAPG-minus-PG update (own~+~peer components) projected onto the direction from the current policy to $(C,C)$. Positive values mean the combined correction directly helps basin entry. Since Phase~L established that the own component contributes negligibly, this is effectively a peer-correction projection with minor own-learning noise. In the central mixed region, the mean projection is 0.0213 and is positive in 94\% of cells (the 13 negative cells all have $|$projection$| < 0.003$, attributable to stochastic estimation noise and the small own component).
