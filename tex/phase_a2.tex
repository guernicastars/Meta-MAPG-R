We decompose the difference between Meta-MAPG and PG update directions into (i) a signed angle-tilt heatmap $\angle(\Delta p^{\mathrm{MM}}) - \angle(\Delta p^{\mathrm{PG}})$ and (ii) a difference quiver $\Delta p^{\mathrm{MM}} - \Delta p^{\mathrm{PG}}$. Mean absolute tilt: $0.254$\,rad. In 22\% of grid cells the tilt is counter-clockwise (positive, toward $(C,C)$), concentrated in the region below the PG separatrix.
