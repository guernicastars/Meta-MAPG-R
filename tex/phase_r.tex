This phase tests the local alignment condition directly. For each Phase~B grid cell we compute the exact one-step peer correction and project its probability-space update onto the direction from the initial policy to $(C,C)$. We then ask whether this scalar predicts cells that fail under PG but succeed under Meta-MAPG.
