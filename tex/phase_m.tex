We sweep the peer coefficient $\lambda$ while keeping the own-learning coefficient fixed. The $\lambda=0$ Meta-MAPG point is therefore the own-correction-only regime, while the dashed baseline is ordinary PG. The best observed setting is $\lambda=5.00$ with basin fraction 63.3\%.
