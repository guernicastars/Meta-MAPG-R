This phase searches a wider symmetric $2\times2$ payoff family with $R=4$ and $P=2$, varying the sucker payoff $S$ and temptation payoff $T$ while keeping $(C,C)$ socially better than miscoordination. The goal is adversarial: find payoff geometries where the peer correction shrinks, rather than expands, the cooperative basin.
